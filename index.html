Who we are

Ronik is a digital studio founded by a group of creative thinkers and makers. We like to try new things. We believe in smart design and progressive technologies. We work closely with forward thinking brands to create engaging user experiences starting with strategy all the way through to development.

Nicole Barth Creative Director

Nicole is a versatile designer with an acute focus on typography and motion graphics. Her unique skills and wide range of design experience add a fundamental layer to creating captivating products.

Roberto Rodriquez Creative Director

Roberto's extensive skills and experience touch all areas of design and technology. As a jack-of-all trades designer, he is able to transform concepts into large scale products. His eagerness to provoke innovation and creative solutions is a foundation for all projects.

Mark Catalano Developer

Mark is a technologist with a broad background in user experience and front-end development. His unique talent is essential in guiding design implementation and ensuring projects are always crafted using progressive technologies.
